Introduction

Reducing food waste is a crucial challenge to the present global food system. Building awareness about problems arising from food waste and educating the public on effective mitigation is the first step towards food wastage.

Further, conducting a waste audit helps determine how to implement changes to minimise food waste. User-friendly food waste audit applications are essential because they can help accurately measure food-related waste, identify waste sources, and evaluate waste reduction strategies' effectiveness
