Sustainable Food Consumption: Making Informed Choices

Food is one of the primary necessities of every human being. People choose different food according to occasion, preferences,
and diet; religion is also a factor for some. According to Pakravan-Charvadeh, M. R., et al., 2022, sustainable food consumption
improves nutrition and food security by only consuming enough food, thus reducing food waste. He also addressed that regional food
consumption utilization is one way to reduce food waste.

Another practical, sustainable food consumption pattern approach is education and awareness through the course or mass media
communication. In Iran, TV programs, radio, and social networks are suitable ways to improve the nutrition culture and
reduce food waste, Pakravan-Charvadeh, M. R. et al., 2022.

It is also worth noting that enough information and warning about too much eating and incorrect food choices will cause diabetes,
heart disease, and obesity. These are likewise the most effective ways to change the current food consumption leading to
sustainable Food Consumption and reducing food waste.
